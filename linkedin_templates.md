## Best Practices

1. **Timing:** Post during business hours (9 AM - 5 PM) for maximum engagement
2. **Length:** Keep posts between 100-300 characters for optimal readability
3. **Hashtags:** Use 3-5 relevant hashtags per post
4. **Engagement:** Respond to comments within 24 hours
5. **Consistency:** Maintain regular posting schedule
6. **Professionalism:** Always maintain a professional, positive tone
7. **Visuals:** Consider adding images when posting manually for higher engagement

---

## Content Guidelines

- ✓ Business-focused and professional
- ✓ Positive and constructive messaging
- ✓ Relevant to industry and audience
- ✓ Clear call-to-action when appropriate
- ✗ No controversial political content
- ✗ No sensitive company information
- ✗ No exaggerated claims or promises
