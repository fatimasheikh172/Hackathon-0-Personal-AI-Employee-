## Usage Guidelines

1. **Character Limit**: All templates under 2200 characters (Instagram limit)
2. **Hashtags**: 10 relevant hashtags per post
3. **Emojis**: Use 2-4 emojis for visual appeal
4. **Timing**: Post during peak hours (9 AM - 11 AM, 7 PM - 9 PM)
5. **Frequency**: Maximum 1-2 posts per day
6. **Images**: Use 1080x1080 square format for best results

## Customization

Replace generic terms with your specific:
- Company name
- Specific metrics/achievements
- Relevant industry hashtags
- Call-to-action links (in bio)
- Tag relevant accounts

## Best Practices

- Engage with comments within 2 hours
- Use Instagram Stories for additional content
- Cross-post to other platforms
- Track engagement metrics
- A/B test different posting times
