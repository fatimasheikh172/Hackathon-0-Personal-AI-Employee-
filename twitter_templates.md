## Usage Guidelines

1. **Character Limit**: All templates under 280 characters
2. **Hashtags**: 3-5 relevant hashtags per tweet
3. **Emojis**: Use sparingly for visual appeal
4. **Timing**: Post during business hours (9 AM - 5 PM)
5. **Frequency**: Maximum 2-3 tweets per day

## Customization

Replace generic terms with your specific:
- Company name
- Specific metrics/achievements
- Relevant industry hashtags
- Call-to-action links
